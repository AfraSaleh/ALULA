Cultural

Archaelological wonders awaited at the heritage sites, a treasure of dwellings, tombs and other sacred places - some still under excavation.

CONCERTS

At the stunning venue Maraya, event-goers were just as taken by the lineup, featuring global stars of the symphony, Latin pop and more.

ART

From Yayoi Kusama's entrancing installation, Brilliance of the Souls, to a light show "performed" by illuminated drones, the art of AlUla was on full display

Authentic Events

Discovered the most authentic ways to experience local culture in AlUla, starting by strolling its market, stocked with handmade wares and delicious food.

Aviation

After touring AlUla on the ground, visitors soared over its sights during a collection of hot-air balloon rides, vintage aircraft flights and more.

SORTS

From three thrilling days of Desert Polo to trail races and hikes for all levels of adventurers, visitors experienced action against the breathtaking backdrop.
